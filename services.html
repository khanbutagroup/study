OUR SERVICES


  • PICK UP FROM THE AIRPORT

    We pick up our students from the airport and we are always with the them until settle to the University of their choice.

  • TRANSPORTATION

    We help our students who wants to study at the University in Istanbul to get "Istanbul Card" which can be used in public transportation such as buses, metro, metrobus, tramway and ferry. But also we help all kinds of transportation for our students who will study in other cities of Turkey.

  • ACCOMMODATION

    We organize the best accommodation which suits the budget of our students, close to the University where our students will study.

  • HEALTH INSURANCE

    We offer health insurance services to our students to benefit from suitable facilities in the state hospitals in Turkey

  • RESIDENCE PERMIT

    We organize to get a residence permit to our students by performing all necessary procedures without having to deal with them.

  • THE BEST UNIVERSITY

  • PROFESSIONAL TEAM

  • DISCOUNT

  • THE CHANCE OF INSTALLMENT PAYMENT

  • ADMISSION TO THE UNIVERSITY - 100% GUARANTEE
